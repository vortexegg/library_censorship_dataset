SLJ Controversial Book Survey: Comments About Age-Appropriateness

by SLJ Staff
Sep 26, 2016 | Filed in News & Features
SLJ's 2016 Controversial Books survey asked school librarians how they determined if a book is age-appropriate. Here's what they said.
RETURN TO THE SELF-CENSORSHIP LANDING PAGE

SLJ's 2016 Controversial Books survey asked school librarians how they determined if a book is age-appropriate for their students.
